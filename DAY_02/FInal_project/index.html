Here is detailed about our College Tech Fest

SRIET Tech Fest 2026 is an annual technical event designed to encourage innovation, collaboration, and practical learning among engineering students. The festival provides participants with opportunities to showcase their technical knowledge, creativity, and problem-solving abilities through various competitions and workshops.

The event features coding contests, web development workshops, AI sessions, project exhibitions, technical quizzes, and expert talks conducted by industry professionals. Students gain valuable exposure to modern technologies while networking with peers and mentors.

Whether you are a beginner eager to learn or an experienced developer looking to challenge yourself, Tech Fest 2026 offers an exciting platform to explore new ideas, enhance your skills, and celebrate technology together.
